DAGYAW

They have clean up drives or what they called "Dagyaw". It is held every week for sustainability of the place head by the baranggay officials together with the locals who lived there. They put signage like "bawal mag tapon ng basura" to keep the place clean and far from waste. They are maintaining a friendly space for their guest and also to avoid harmful situations that can affect their guest and also to the ecosystem."
